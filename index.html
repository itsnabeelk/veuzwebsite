NEXT PROFILE

Next Trading Establishment is a company specialized in the field of trading, established in 2020 and has a wide background in commercial fields, import and export in many fields such as exporting and importing clothes, industrial materials supply and other high-quality

products, and it also has a founding base in many different fields.The company’s headquarters is located in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, and we are working to continue our mission to cover many regions all over the world.

“Next Trading Establishment” carries out commercial transactions through Import and export in many fields such as clothes and T-shirts and industrial materials supply of Interior designs, web and social media design, Video shooting, branding and stationery supplies.

GOALS & OBJECTIVES

We always aim to maintain our position in the commercial field and the business market by gaining the trust of customers, obtaining excellent business deals and ensuring the provision of high quality products and reliable services. Supported by proactive Marketing and promotion, that far exceeds client expectations.

Mission

Our mission lies in developing the concept of trade, ensuring permanent competition in the commercial labour market and developing our services to suit all customers’ needs through professional and ethical partnerships with our partners in all Arab and foreign countries, with a double interest in consulting and training through continuous interaction between Arab and international expertise.

Vision

Our vision is summarized in its constant quest to expand to cover the Gulf Cooperation Council countries and maintain its position in the commercial labour market. We also seek to create a distinguished position for our customers by working constantly to meet all their needs and provide high quality products.
